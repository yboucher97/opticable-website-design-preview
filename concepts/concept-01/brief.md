# Concept 01 Brief

## Positioning

Concept 01 broadens Opticable from a security-first commercial contractor into a more complete technology infrastructure partner for businesses, commercial properties, and multi-unit environments.

The concept should feel modern, technological, professional, human, accessible, and credible for business buyers.

## Visual direction

- clean white and light-gray surfaces
- green-led palette based on the Opticable logo
- darker anthracite text and framing
- more breathing room and clearer page rhythm
- polished service cards with stronger visual hierarchy
- a modern tech-business tone instead of a narrow low-voltage or security tone

## Homepage goals

- lead with "Votre partenaire en infrastructure technologique."
- explain Opticable as a partner for design, installation, and management of technology infrastructure
- spotlight five key offers:
  - cablage structure
  - Wi-Fi professionnel
  - solutions informatiques
  - systemes de surveillance par camera
  - intercom et controle d acces
- add a stronger "Pourquoi choisir Opticable" section
- keep a premium, fluid, trustworthy layout

## Service direction

The concept should keep the existing service architecture but rebalance the messaging around:

- structured cabling and backbone infrastructure
- professional Wi-Fi design and deployment
- broader IT solutions and operational support
- surveillance systems
- access control and intercom integration

The `network-infrastructure` service acts as the broader "Solutions informatiques" offer in this concept.

## Copy direction

- use the user prompt as the base source of truth
- preserve what is already strong in the current site where useful
- keep bilingual output coherent even if French is the lead language for this concept
- prefer clear, confident business language over SEO-heavy phrasing
